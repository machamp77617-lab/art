Grok, Is This True?

Top Secret Files

Year
2025
Material
Safe, 34.7 × 19 × 24.7 cm, with encrypted external drive

A grey electronic safe sits on the floor of the exhibition space, digital keypad and keyhole facing the viewer. Inside is an encrypted external drive containing the generation logs for the AI-generated pseudo-investigative report. This double encryption digital plus physical puts the evidence into a strange state: it exists and does not exist at the same time.

You can see the safe, read its serial number, but you will never open it. What is locked inside is not money but the secret of how fabricated conspiracy theories were generated.

Conversational AI operates the same way. You ask a question, you get an answer. You do not see the training data, the algorithmic filtering, the editorial decisions baked into the model. The black box produces outputs, but the process remains inaccessible. The safe makes this enclosure visible. The process is locked away, just as platform algorithms remain locked away. That inaccessibility becomes the work itself.

Point of Interest

You Are Here

Year
2025
Material
London map, 101.6 × 77 cm, red stickers, perforated suspension

One is a regular London map covered with red stickers. Each red dot sticker represents the location of a billboard. These billboard locations are not exact but represent the feeling that your world is filled with them, those places that affect your attention. I call this method Perceptual Cartography.

I set up a viewing mechanism for the work. I prepared a light wand. When the audience scans the back of the map with the light wand, they can see the specific locations covered under the red marks.

"Through the method of perceptual cartography, I mark the 'presence' of billboards in the city rather than their exact positions. This map does not pursue completeness finding all billboards but pursues authenticity: conveying the 'ubiquitous' visual violence experience. Each marked point represents not a billboard, but the public visual experience occupied by advertising."
